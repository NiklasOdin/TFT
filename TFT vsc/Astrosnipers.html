Composition overview

Here is a simple overview of what the ideal composition is at level 8 in which your primary carries are Teemo and Jhin who you also want to prioritize items on. This foundation of this composition is not built from the start, which implies that it is a pivot comp. A pivot comp is when you switch the majority of your units for other units in order to completely change all your synergies, this is done when most of the core champions are not available in the early levels.

Early game

Ashe, Nautilus, Karma and Blitzcrank are the only champions you will be able to find consistently during early levels. You should therefore accumilate them throughout the entire game, either putting them in your early comp or keeping them on the bench until you make the full pivot. In the very early stages it is suggested to run what is the strongest at that moment. For example if you get a lot of Cybernetics you can run Leona with a vanguard as frontline and a Vayne who holds your Jhin items. Example below.

Champions

Do not reroll at all until you are at least level 7 as the chance to hit your core units such as Teemo, Gnar, Jhin and Wukong are very low due to all of them being 4 cost units. You should instead build up interest and only spend gold on XP and units through levels 1-7 while trying to get to level 8 as fast as possible.
