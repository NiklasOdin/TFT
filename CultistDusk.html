How to play

This composition focuses on building around Jhin and Riven, who are also the main carries that you itemize in this build. Depending on your chosen champion your comp will change quite a bit, but the chosen champions you should always buy are:

• Riven with Dusk/Keeper as chosen trait

• Jhin with Cultist/Sharpshooter as chosen trait

• Any champion in the build with dusk trait (preferably Riven or Cassiopeia)

In this composition going level 7 as fast as you can is ideal, but depending on your health situation this might change. For example if you are winning a lot, it is much better to go level 8 untill you reroll for your champions since you are much more likely to hit your core champions at level 8.
